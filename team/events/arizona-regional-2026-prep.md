# Arizona Regional 2026 — Prep Guide

tags: #competition #2026 #regional #firstinspires

## Know Before You Go

**Event:** Arizona North FIRST Robotics Competition (Reefscape)  
**Location:** NAU Dome, Flagstaff, AZ  
**Dates:** March 11–14, 2026

This guide covers expectations and logistics for the competition.

---

## Core Expectations

### Gracious Professionalism
- Be respectful to other teams, mentors, volunteers, and organizers
- Support and help others succeed
- Celebrate the spirit of robotics and competition

### FIRST Codes & Compliance
- **E102 & G201:** Safety and technical rules — read them before competition
- **YPP Reporting:** Yellow card/red card system — understand the rules to avoid penalties
- All teams must follow FIRST Robotics guidelines

---

## Competition Essentials

### Schedule & Events
- **Practice Matches:** Thursday, March 12 (8 AM – 6 PM)
- **Qualification Matches:** Friday–Saturday, March 13–14 (8 AM – 6 PM)
- **Finals:** Saturday, March 14 (afternoon)
- **Pit Teardown:** Saturday, March 14 (6 PM – 8 PM)

### Load In/Out Process
- **Wednesday, 5 PM:** Load robot into pit at NAU Dome
- **Saturday, 6 PM:** Begin pit teardown
- Expect check-in procedures and pit assignment process

### Pit Layout & Resources
- Pit spaces are assigned — check your team's location upon arrival
- Machine shop access available for emergency repairs
- Power, compressed air, and common tools typically provided

---

## Staying Ready

### What to Bring
- FIRST registration & ID
- Team notebook (engineering/design documentation)
- Spare parts kit
- Laptops/devices (bring chargers!)
- Team shirts/safety gear
- Food & water (competitions are long!)

### Arizona Resources
- Flagstaff area shops for last-minute supplies
- Weather: March in Flagstaff is cool (40s–50s°F) — bring layers
- Things to do: historic downtown Flagstaff, local restaurants

### Between Matches
- Pit maintenance & strategy discussions
- Debrief performance data
- Manage morale & team energy
- Stay hydrated & fueled

---

## Quick Reminders

✅ Know the game rules (Reefscape)

✅ Stay organized in pit  
✅ Respect other teams & volunteers  
✅ Document issues & successes for improvement
