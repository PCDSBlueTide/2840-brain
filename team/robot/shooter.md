# Shooter System

tags: #robot #shooter

## Design
- Hooded turret shooter
- Turret swivels **side-to-side** (Limelight 3A mounted on swivel)
- Up/down motion is separate; Limelight NOT mounted to up/down axis

## Components
- 2" REV ION compliance wheels (×28 ordered)
- HTD timing belt + plastic timing pulleys
- Linear servos — map desired flywheel angle to servo position

## Prototyping
- AndyMark Launcher-in-a-Box considered for early prototyping
- 1" compression being tested (balls less compressible than 2020 game)

## Status (Feb 22, 2026)
- Turret base plate CNC'd ✅
- Turret subsystem (software) in progress on separate branch
- Hole alignment issue on CNC'd parts under investigation
